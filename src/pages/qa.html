Dareboost

Dareboost is designed for triggering insightful audit of the website on quality and performance . DareBoost detects the root causes of slowdowns and provides tailor-made suggestions to fix them. It provides a comprehensive report of the website performance and quality, with real browser metrics and top of the range tips to drive optimization in a few seconds. Dareboost also carry out website monitoring and speed comparison for competitive advantage.
